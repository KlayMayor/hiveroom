🍯What is HiveRoom?

HiveRoom is a social interior platform where you decorate isometric rooms on a hexagonal tile map and buy/sell tiles using the HIVE token economy.
Build your space, grow it, and gather with community friends to form a village!

🚀Getting Started
1
Google Login — Click the LOG IN button in the top right to sign in with your Google account
2
300 HIVE Welcome Bonus — 300 HIVE is automatically granted on first login
3
Click an Empty Tile — Click any empty tile on the map to open the tile purchase panel
4
Purchase Tile — Pay the ZONE fee to open the room editor
5
Decorate Your Room — Choose wallpaper, flooring, furniture, decorations, and items
6
Register on Map — Enter your Twitter(X) ID and interest, then your room appears on the HiveRoom map
💡 Room creation cost varies by ZONE. You cannot create a room if your balance is insufficient!

💡 Drag items freely to position them anywhere in the room!
💰HIVE Economy
🎁 Daily Wage

Every day at UTC 00:00, all users automatically receive 10 HIVE.
Check the header timer for the next payout countdown.

📅 Check-in

Find a Reserved tile on the map and click it.
Solve a simple math quiz to earn 5 HIVE.
Once per tile per day (UTC reset).

🏠 Create Room

Creating a room deducts the ZONE fee and stores it as tile value.
After purchase, other users cannot buy it for 36 hours.

📈 Tile Investment

Use the Invest button on your room panel to add HIVE and increase tile value.
Higher value means buyers must pay more.

🔄 Buy a Tile

Click another user's room → confirm purchase amount → buy.
HIVE is automatically sent to the seller upon completion.
A 1-hour cooldown applies after purchase.

🏦 Monthly Tax

On the 1st of each month at UTC 00:00, owned tile values decrease by ZONE.
Tile value will not drop below 0.
(Invest HIVE to maintain tile value and avoid being sold off.)

!! You cannot prevent other users from buying your room. Invest HIVE to raise tile value and protect your room.
✅ If the displayed price differs from the actual price (due to investment), the purchase will be rejected and the latest price shown. Click Buy again to confirm.
🏘️Room Management
✏️ Edit Room

Click your room on the map → Edit Room button, or use the profile menu → Edit Room to update your interior anytime.

🚚 Move Room

Use the Move button on your room panel to relocate to another empty tile.
Moving cost is the difference between the old and new tile prices (charged or refunded).

🏦 Estate Management

Profile menu → My Estate to view current values of all your rooms and invest HIVE into any of them.

🔒Reserved Tiles

Some tiles are designated as Reserved and cannot be purchased.
Click a Reserved tile to reveal the Check-in button.
Find Reserved tiles across the map to earn 5 HIVE each day! There are 27 Reserved tiles in total. 🍯

💡 Check-in is available once per Reserved tile per day (UTC reset).
🐦Twitter(X) ID & Profile

• The Twitter ID entered when registering appears as a circular profile badge on the map
• Hover over the avatar to see @twitterID; click to open the X profile
• Enter an Interest Twitter ID to show a small interest badge at the bottom-right of the avatar
• Owner avatar and interest badge are also visible in the room panel
• A Twitter ID already used by another user cannot be registered

⏳Purchase Cooldown
Situation Cooldown Scope
After creating a room on empty tile 36h Tile (others cannot buy)
After buying a filled room 1h Tile (others cannot buy)

During cooldown, the Buy button is disabled and remaining time is shown.

🖱️Map Controls

• Mouse Wheel — Zoom in / out
• Click & Drag — Pan the map
• Click empty tile — Open tile purchase panel
• Click filled room — Open room info & buy panel
• Click avatar badge — Open that user's Twitter(X) profile
• 🔎 Tile Search — Filter and highlight tiles
• ⌂ — Reset map to default position

🔗Room URL Sharing

After registering your room, share it on Twitter with the POST to X button.
URL format: hiveroom.vercel.app/#room=NUMBER&zoom=2
Visiting this link automatically centers the map on that room.
